Who We Are

ITCLUB Foundation is a value-driven, not-for-profit organization dedicated to advancing India’s national development goals.

With a focus on IT, Artificial Intelligence, and digital innovation, we empower youth, women, and marginalized populations through long-term, impact-oriented programs.

Registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, we tackle critical challenges by fostering strategic partnerships with CSR organizations to build a digitally empowered society.
